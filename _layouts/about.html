Supervised and Semi-supervised Multi-Structure Segmentation and Landmark Detection in Dental Data. A joint event at MICCAI 2024 featuring three dental data challenges: ToothFairy2 for multi-structure segmentation in CBCT volumes, 3DTeethLand24 for 3D teeth landmark detection, and a semi-supervised teeth segmentation challenge.
This half-day event will offer an introduction to maxillofacial image segmentation and landmark detection, followed by individual sessions highlighting the results of each challenge. The event will take place on October 6th in Marrakesh, Morocco, offering key insights into cutting-edge dental AI techniques.

Multi-Structure and Multi-Modal Learning for 3D Dental Image Analysis. The ToothFairy3 Workshop, a CVPR 2025 event, focuses on the latest challenges and advancements in 3D dental image analysis, specifically targeting techniques for CBCT and IOS modalities. The event brings together researchers, industry professionals, and practitioners to explore innovative solutions in areas like image registration, instance segmentation, and landmark detection.
