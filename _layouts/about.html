ToothFairy: A Cone-Beam Computed Tomography Segmentation Challenge. The ToothFairy Challenge, initiated by the University of Modena and Reggio Emilia (UNIMORE) in collaboration with Radboud University Medical Center, focuses on the segmentation of the Inferior Alveolar Canal from CBCT scans of dental images. The challenge aims to foster the development of innovative AI-driven solutions for dental imaging analysis, encouraging international collaboration among academic institutions, healthcare professionals, and researchers. Results from the challenge were presented at MICCAI 2023, featuring contributions from over 30 research groups worldwide. Prizes were awarded to the top three best-performing solutions.

Supervised and Semi-supervised Multi-Structure Segmentation and Landmark Detection in Dental Data. The ToothFairy 2 Challenge, launched by the University of Modena and Reggio Emilia (UNIMORE) in collaboration with Radboud University Medical Center, focuses on multi-structure segmentation of CBCT maxillo-facial images. The goal is to segment various anatomical structures, including the Background, Jaws, Inferior Alveolar Canals, Maxillary Sinus, Pharynx, Bridges, Crowns, Implants, and Upper and Lower Teeth, while fostering international collaboration. Results from the challenge were presented at MICCAI 2024, featuring contributions from 26 research groups worldwide. Prizes were awarded to the top three best-performing solutions.
